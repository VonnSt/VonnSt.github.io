Poems by:
Lyndon and Heath

Name of Poem: #Aspire

Poet: Heath

Aspire, dream, reach for the sky,
Don't let any possibilities pass you bye,
Let God lead you along and your aspirations keep you strong,
With this combination nothing can possibly go wrong,
Aspire to make the world a better place,
Aspire even for your sins to be erased,
Aspire to win an academic race,
To be smart instead of day dreaming in space,
Aspire to make an impact on the world,
To donate clothes to every boy and girl,
Walk the road that leads to success,
Achieve your goals and nothing less,
Expect nothing other than your very best,
Aspire and trust that God will handle the rest.
